Welcome to the Wonderful World of Harry Potter

  • Harry Potter Books

    The Harry Potter book series consists of seven books, the first in the series being Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, released in 1997, and the last book in the series being Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, released in 2007. In 2016, a follow up book was released, namely Harry Potter and the Cursed Child. There have also been two spin off books released, which are Fantastic Beasts and Where To Find Them and Harry Potter: The Tales of Beedle the Bard. For more information on the Harry Potter book series, please go to the 'Books' section.
