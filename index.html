Welcome to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter


  • About

    Harry Potter is a brilliant series of fantasy books about an orphaned wizard, Harry Potter, his two best friends Hermione Granger and Ronald Weasley, and their endless adventures at their school of Witchcraft and Wizardy, Hogwarts. For more information on this wonderful chronicle, please go to the 'About' section.


  • Harry Potter Books

    The Harry Potter book series consists of seven books, the first in the series being Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone, released in 1997, and the last book in the series being Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, released in 2007. In 2016, a follow up book was released, namely Harry Potter and the Cursed Child. There have also been two spin off books released, which are Fantastic Beasts and Where To Find Them and Harry Potter: The Tales of Beedle the Bard. For more information on the Harry Potter book series, please go to the 'Books' section.


  • Harry Potter Movies

    The Harry Potter movie series were produced and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. It is made up of eight fantasy films, the first being Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (released in 2001) and the last being Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 (released in 2011). For more information on the Harry Potter movie series, please go to the 'Movies' section.


  • Harry Potter Characters

    Throughout the Harry Potter series, we are introduced to numerous characters that each have a role to play in the story. The most highlighted characters are the main character Harry Potter, his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, his wise principal Professor Dumbledore and his archnemesis Voldemort (also referred to as Tom Marvolo Riddle). For a more detailed outline on individual Harry Potter characters, please go to the 'Characters' section.


  • The Author

    The creator behind the famous Harry Potter series is British author Joanne Kathleen Rowling, more commonly referred to by her pen name J.K. Rowling. For more detailed background information on J.K. Rowling, please go to the 'Author' section.


  • Harry Potter Fan Club

    For all those interested in the Harry Potter franchise and all the magic it entails, there is an official fan club that one can become an exclusive member of. The fan club provides infinite quizzes, magical features, puzzles, videos, and so much more! For more information on the Harry Potter fan club, please go to the 'Fan Club' section.
