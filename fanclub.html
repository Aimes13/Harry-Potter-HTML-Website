Welcome to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter


Harry Potter Fan Club

For all those interested in the Harry Potter franchise and all the magic it entails, there is an official fan club that one can become an exclusive member of. The fan club provides infinite quizzes, magical features, puzzles, videos, and so much more!


  • Wizarding World

    Here you can delve deeper into J.K. Rowling's stories with articles on wizarding world themes, from a broad range of authors offering their thoughts; test your knowledge with a range of quizzes; enjoy exclusive new videos, and get up to speed with the latest Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts news. The official Harry Potter Fan Club can also be joined and viewed exclusively through the Wizarding World website. The Harry Potter Fan Club is a official American-British Club which gives fans experiences of the Wizarding World, including a Fan Club newsletter, member benefits and a membership experience: Wizarding World gold.


  • Harry Potter Wiki

    The Harry Potter Wiki is an online encyclopaedia for information on J. K. Rowling's Wizarding World — including information on all the Harry Potter novels, films, and video games, and all the expanded universe, including but not limited to, Pottermore, Harry Potter and the Cursed Child, and the Fantastic Beasts series.
