  • Harry Potter Characters

    Throughout the Harry Potter series, we are introduced to numerous characters that each have a role to play in the story. The most highlighted characters are the main character Harry Potter, his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, his wise principal Professor Dumbledore and his archnemesis Voldemort (also referred to as Tom Marvolo Riddle). For a more detailed outline on individual Harry Potter characters, please go to the 'Characters' section.
