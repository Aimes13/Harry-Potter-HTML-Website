Welcome to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter


About

  • Harry Potter is a brilliant series of fantasy books about an orphan, Harry

  • He lives with his abusive aunt and uncle, Vernon and Petunia Dursley and their bullying son, Dudley

  • On his eleventh birthday, Harry discovers he is a wizard when Rubeus Hagrid delivers him an acceptance letter to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

  • The series follows Harry, his two best friends Hermione Granger and Ronald Weasley, and their endless adventures at Hogwarts

  • The series also highlights the battles Harry faces against his enemy Voldemort, every step of the way
